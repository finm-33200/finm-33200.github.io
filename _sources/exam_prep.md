# Exam Preparation

This page provides a running outline of what will be covered on each exam.

## Midterm Exam

The midterm covers material from **Weeks 1--4**, including all lecture notes, in-class discussions, and homework assignments **HW 0 through HW 3**.

### Online Notes

We may not cover every page of the online notes during class. **All material in the course notes is fair game for the midterm and final exams**, including pages not explicitly discussed in class. You are responsible for reading the full notes on your own.

### Required "Reading" from Homework Assignments

Each homework assignment includes a Required "Reading" section. All material from those sections (HW 0 through HW 3) may appear on the midterm.
